Control Transfer Statements

There are 4 control transfer statements in Swift. We have already looked at 'continue' and 'break'. We looked at how we can use these to control the flow of execution in for, while and do while.

continue

Usage of 'continue' in all kinds of loops for, while and do while is covered in previous chapter. Swift Chapter 12 Control Flow for, while and do-while

break

Usage of 'break' in all kinds of loops has been covered in Swift Chapter 12 Control Flow for, while and do-while

'break' can also be used to exit the current switch block and start execution after the closing brace of 'switch' statement/block.

fallthrough

As discussed previously in the beginning of 'Switch' explanation, switch does not have the implicit fallthrough as with Objective-C, or C. So in case you wanted to verify for the next case statement after the current case statement, we use fallthrough keyword to avoid exiting switch block.
